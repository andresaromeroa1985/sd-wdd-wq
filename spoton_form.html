SpotOn Website Services — Terms & Conditions

1. Subscription & Billing. Your SpotOn website subscription is billed monthly. The first charge occurs when you approve the start of the website build process.

2. Included pages. Your subscription includes up to 6 custom-designed pages. Additional pages are charged at $50 USD per page, subject to your written approval before work begins.

3. Content ownership. You confirm that you own or have the legal right to use all text, images, logos, and other content you provide to SpotOn for use on your website.

4. Logo design. If you request a logo ($99 standard / $299 custom), a proposal will be sent for your review and signature before any work or charges begin.

5. Domain. If SpotOn registers a domain on your behalf, it remains your property. Upon cancellation, the domain will be transferred back to you at no cost.

6. Revisions. Your subscription includes 2 revision rounds per month (up to 3 line items per revision). Additional revision rounds are available at $75 USD each.

7. Auto-publishing. If you select auto-publishing, SpotOn will attempt to reach you at least 5 times before publishing your site.

8. Cancellation. You may cancel at any time by emailing [email protected]. If you cancel after design work has begun without an active subscription, a $350 design fee may apply.

9. Support. Post-launch edits are handled by our support team. SpotOn provides support Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm EST.
